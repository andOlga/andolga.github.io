&Olga's project list

We are &Olga. This page is a list of the projects we worked on.

These are our own projects (all public domain):

  • The Umineko song searcher
  • A tool to automatically add thcrap-patched Touhou games to Steam (broken & no longer maintained, archived for reference)
  • A "single-player" chat application for plural folks and chatfic writers
  • A glyph converter for Heartbound
  • A counter widget for OBS
  • A music player web-source for OBS that displays info about tracks as they're played
  • A thing that repeats what you say back to you in a computer voice
  • Some userscripts
