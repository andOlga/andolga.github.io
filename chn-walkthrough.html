The Choice System

At different points in Ch√§oS;HEAd NoAH, you will come into contact with a unique choice system. Through it, you can choose if the protagonist reacts positively, negaitvely, or neutrally to the current situation.

Click the green button or press Left Trigger on controller for positive; click the red button or press Right Trigger for negative; or continue reading normally for neutral.

You can change your mind for a few lines before the choice is "locked in". To undo your choice and revert to neutral, simply press the opposite of the button you pressed originally (i.e. if you pressed green, press red, and vice versa). Then you can either stay on the neutral course or re-do your choice.

Every route in the game also has a single set of YES/NO questions that must be answered correctly to "lock in" the route. It's recommended to figure the answers to these questions out on your own. It will instantly be clear whether you got them right or wrong. However, if you are completely stuck at one of these sections, you will find the answers at the bottom of this walkthrough, in the "YES/NO answers" section.
